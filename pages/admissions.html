Admissions

Welcome to the Admissions page of VIT University. Here, you'll find information about our admission process, requirements, and important dates.

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Standardized test scores (if applicable)
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement

How to Apply?

To apply for admission to VIT University, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Wait for admission decision.

Important Dates


  • Application Deadline: January 15, 2024
  • Admission Decision Notification: February 15, 2024
  • Orientation Day: March 1, 2024
  • First Day of Classes: March 15, 2024
