Research

Current Research Projects

Explore some of our ongoing research initiatives:


  • Sustainable Urban Transportation Solutions: This research project aims to explore and develop sustainable transportation solutions for urban environments. It includes an analysis of public transportation systems, electric mobility options, and their impact on reducing carbon emissions and congestion.
  • Genomic Mapping of Cancer Mutations: This project involves mapping the genomic mutations associated with various types of cancer. Researchers will analyze the genetic basis of cancer development, identify potential biomarkers, and explore personalized treatment options.
  • Social Media and Mental Health: This longitudinal study investigates the relationship between social media usage patterns and mental health outcomes among adolescents. It involves surveying participants over several years to assess the impact of social media on well-being.
